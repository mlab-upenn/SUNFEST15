Column Descriptions:

1) year
2) month
3) day of month
4) hour of day
5) Outside Air Temp (C)
6) Outside Air Temp (F)
7) DewPoint
8) Relative Humidity
9) Incident Solar Radiation
10) Wind Speed
11) Gusts Speed
12) Wind Direction
13) HDD: Heating Degree Days
14) CDD: Cooling Degree Days
15) Occupancy
16) Building Area
17) Value

The "5" in the file name means steam demand, "6" means chilled water demand, and "2" means electricity demand.
